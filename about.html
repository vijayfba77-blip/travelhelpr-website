About Travel Helpr

Making every journey feel simpler, safer, and more meaningful.

Why Travel Helpr Exists

Travel Helpr was born from a simple idea — that planning a trip should feel exciting, not overwhelming. Whether you’re visiting a city for the first time or returning to a familiar place, the questions remain the same: Where should I stay? How should I get around? What should I not miss? Is it safe for my family?

That’s where Travel Helpr steps in — your AI-powered companion that simplifies decisions and helps you explore with confidence. We combine data-driven insights with a human touch, making every trip feel personal, well-planned, and safe.

Our Mission

To empower travelers with meaningful information and real-time insights that help them make smarter decisions, stay safe, and truly enjoy every moment of their journey. We believe travel isn’t just about reaching places — it’s about connecting, learning, and growing.

Meet the Founder

Vijay Kumar – Founder, Travel Helpr

Vijay’s idea for Travel Helpr came from his own travel experiences — especially the challenges of navigating new cities, planning efficiently, and ensuring safety while traveling with family. After years of working in technology and global delivery roles, he decided to build something that bridges the gap between technology and travel empathy. Travel Helpr is his vision to make every traveler feel prepared, confident, and cared for — no matter where the journey leads.
